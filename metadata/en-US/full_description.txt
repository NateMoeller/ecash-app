Ecash App is a Fedimint wallet that supports Lightning, On-chain, and Ecash payments. It is built with Flutter and Rust, and is fully free and open source.

Key features:

* Join multiple Fedimint federations and manage them in one wallet
* Send and receive Lightning payments via LNv1 and LNv2
* Send and receive on-chain Bitcoin
* Mint and redeem Ecash tokens
* Claim a Lightning Address for easy receiving
* Connect external apps via Nostr Wallet Connect (NWC)
* Discover federations via Nostr
* Back up and recover your wallet using a seed phrase
* Privacy-focused with no tracking or analytics
